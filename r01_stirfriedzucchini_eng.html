Make the sauce

Take out ingredients → ✅ Make the sauce → prep ingredients → cook
★ Mix all ingredients below in a cup with a spoon ★
Sugar 1T
Soy sauce 8T
Vinegar 2T

Prep ingredients

Take out ingredients → make the sauce → ✅ Prep ingredients → cook
💧 Wash 2 zucchinis
Julienne it into strips about 0.2 inches thick
Put in frying pan
Add salt 4 pinches
Add perilla oil 2T and cooking oil 1T

Cook

Take out ingredients → make the sauce → prep ingredients → ✅ Cook
Stir-fry ingredients in the pan with a silicone spatula
🔥🔥 Medium heat
Cook until onions and zucchinis are more than half cooked and translucent
When onions and zucchinis are partially cooked, add the sauce and continue stir-frying
🔥🔥 Medium heat
When everything is cooked, turn off the heat and it's done!
★ Enjoy! ★
