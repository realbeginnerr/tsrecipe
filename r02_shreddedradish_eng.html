Shopping List

  • 1 radish

Take out the ingredients

✅ Take out the ingredients → Make the sauce → Prep the ingredients → Mix
① Upper shelf
  • Automatic shredder (or manual shredder)
  • 2 plastic gloves
  • 1 spoon
  • Side dish container (large enough to hold the salad)
② Lower shelf
  • Shredder (for peeling radish)
  • Scale
  • Salt, Sugar
  • Vinegar (or plum extract)
  • MSG (optional)
③ Lower shelf
  • Large bowl
  • Wide tray
  • Cutting board, Knife
④ Refrigerator
  • 1/2 radish
  • Red pepper flakes (coarse kimchi pepper flakes. Fine pepper flakes are okay)
  • Anchovy sauce (fish sauce is okay)
⑤ Freezer
  • Chives (green onions are okay)
  • 2T minced garlic
  • 1/2T minced ginger

Prep the ingredients

Take out the ingredients → ✅ Prep the ingredients → Make the sauce → Mix
Plug in automatic shredder and place tray in front
💧Wash chives
💧Wash dirt off radish
Cut off top with knife and peel skin with manual shredder
💧Wash off small remaining skin pieces
Weigh
(This recipe is based on 800-850g. Adjust seasoning amounts if using more or less)
Shred with automatic shredder
Put in large bowl
Add 1T salt, 2T sugar to bowl
Put on plastic gloves and mix
Set 15-minute timer
Cut 3 chives into 5cm lengths

Make the sauce

Take out the ingredients → Prep the ingredients → ✅ Make the sauce → Mix
★ Mix these ingredients in the side dish container with a spoon ★
2T red pepper flakes
2T minced garlic
1/2T sugar
1T vinegar
(or plum extract)
2T anchovy sauce
1/2T minced ginger
1/4T MSG
(optional)
Add cut chives
Mix well with spoon
When 15-minute timer rings, put on gloves and mix a few times
Set 15-minute timer again
When 15-minute timer rings, put on gloves and squeeze out excess water
After squeezing, let it sit in the side dish container for a while
Rinse the bowl used for salting

Mix

Take out the ingredients → Prep the ingredients → Make the sauce → ✅ Mix
Put salted radish and seasoning in bowl
Mix well with gloves on
Put in side dish container and let ferment at room temperature for one day before eating
(Can be refrigerated immediately if preferred)
★ Enjoy! ★
